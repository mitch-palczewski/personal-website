Play: Simplify on itch.io

The objective of the game is to see as an artist and exercise your perceptual ability. Simplification is a powerful tool, but it is not necessarily intuitive. Through a series of artworks and photographs you will investigate different ways to simplify images and you will be challenged to reconstruct the work.
